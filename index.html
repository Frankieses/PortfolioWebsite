About Me

Living in Antequera Bohol, I am Dave Francis Baugbog, a third-year BSIT student at BISU Balilihan Campus. As the Head Photojournalist at CAMPUSS ACCESS, I skillfully capture the dynamic essence of both campus and external life through my lens. Whether documenting campus events or exploring the beauty of various areas in Bohol, photography allows me to weave stories that transcend words, providing a visual narrative that resonates with the community.
